Instructions
  • You will now play our game, Space Oddity. There are three levels to play. Each level takes 3 minutes. You need to survive the 3 minutes to proceed to the next level. If you lose midway, you are requested to restart that level. You will finish playing the game when you survive all three levels.
  • Play with sound on! There are sound effects that we want you to hear while you play.
  • The game can take some time to load depending on the machine you play on. However if it takes more than 30 seconds to load, refresh the page.

    • Attention Chrome Users

  • If you use Chrome, please ensure 'hardware acceleration' is enabled (this is turned on by default). If this it not enabled, the game will have critical performance issues. To enable 'hardware acceleration':
      • 1. In your URL bar, navigate to chrome://settings/system
      2. Ensure 'Use hardware acceleration when available' is enabled. If not, please enable it (you can disable it again after finishing the study).
      3. Re-launch your browser and navigate back to this study.
